Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal data

Last updated: 11 May 2026

Gaia Search Ltd is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use and store your information in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who we are

Gaia Search Ltd is the data controller for personal data collected through this website and our recruitment activities.

  • Company name: Gaia Search Ltd
  • Company registration: 15162203
  • Contact email: shaun.silva@gaia-search.com
  • Website: www.gaia-search.com

If you have any questions about how we handle your data, please contact us at the email above.

2. What data we collect

We may collect and process the following personal data:

From candidates

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Professional sector and years of experience
  • Any information you provide in your message or CV
  • Job application history and correspondence

From clients

  • Name and job title
  • Business email address and phone number
  • Company name and details
  • Recruitment requirements and correspondence

From website visitors

  • Website usage data via Google Analytics (anonymised, including pages visited, time on site, and general location)
  • IP address (processed by Google Analytics)

3. How we collect your data

We collect personal data in the following ways:

  • When you submit the CV registration form on our website
  • When you email us directly
  • When you contact us via WhatsApp or LinkedIn
  • When we source your details from professional networking platforms (such as LinkedIn) in the course of our recruitment activities
  • Through Google Analytics when you browse our website

4. Why we use your data and our legal basis

We only process your personal data where we have a valid legal basis to do so under UK GDPR:

Legitimate interests

We process candidate and client data to carry out our recruitment services — matching candidates to suitable roles and helping clients find the right talent. This is in the legitimate interests of all parties involved.

Consent

Where you have ticked the consent box on our registration form, we process your data on the basis of your consent. You may withdraw consent at any time by emailing us.

Contract

Where we have a contractual relationship with a client or candidate, we process data as necessary to fulfil that contract.

Legal obligation

We may process data where required by law, for example to comply with tax or employment regulations.

5. How we store your data

Your personal data may be stored in the following places:

  • Our email inbox (Microsoft Outlook or Google Gmail)
  • A CRM system or spreadsheet used to manage candidate and client records
  • Google Analytics (anonymised website usage data)

We take reasonable steps to ensure your data is stored securely and only accessible to authorised personnel.

6. How long we keep your data

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary:

  • Candidates: Up to 2 years from last contact, unless you ask us to delete it sooner or we place you in a role
  • Clients: For the duration of our business relationship and up to 6 years after for legal and tax purposes
  • Website analytics: As per Google Analytics default retention settings (up to 26 months)

After these periods, your data will be securely deleted or anonymised.

7. Who we share your data with

We do not sell your personal data. We will only share your data in the following circumstances:

  • With clients: With your explicit consent, we may share your CV and relevant details with prospective employers as part of the recruitment process
  • With clients: Client contact details may be shared internally for the purposes of managing that relationship
  • Google Analytics: Anonymised website usage data is processed by Google. See Google's Privacy Policy
  • Legal requirements: Where required by law or a regulatory authority

We will always inform you before sharing your CV with any third party employer.

8. Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: You can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure: You can ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing: You can ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability: You can ask for your data in a portable format
  • Right to object: You can object to us processing your data on the basis of legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at shaun.silva@gaia-search.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies and analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to collect anonymised data about your visit.

No personally identifiable information is collected through Google Analytics. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

10. Links to other websites

Our website contains links to LinkedIn and other external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to read their privacy policies.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

12. How to complain

If you are unhappy with how we have handled your personal data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK's data protection regulator:

Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)
Website: ico.org.uk
Helpline: 0303 123 1113

We would, however, always appreciate the chance to address your concerns directly before you contact the ICO — please email us first at shaun.silva@gaia-search.com.
