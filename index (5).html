About Gaia Search

Meet the Founders

Gaia Search was founded in 2023 on a simple belief - that progress in environmental consulting and clean energy starts with the right people in the right roles.

Shaun spends his days speaking with clients and candidates across the UK, getting to know the people behind the CVs and the teams behind the projects. He cares a lot about the sector, and even more about making sure the right people end up in the right roles (not just the fastest fill).

Of course, every founder story has someone who actually runs things… and in this case, that's Brendan.

Brendan is Shaun's loyal companion and self-appointed Chief Morale Officer. He doesn't read job specs or write shortlists, but he's very involved in the important stuff, whether it be organising walks, keeping a track on snack levels, and making sure nobody takes themselves too seriously for too long. He's also a strong believer that all good ideas are best discussed while throwing a tennis ball.

Between them, Shaun and Brendan keep things grounded- one focused on building a specialist recruitment business in environmental consulting and clean energy, the other making sure there's always a reason to step outside and reset.
