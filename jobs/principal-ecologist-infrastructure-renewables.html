Principal Ecologist — Infrastructure & Renewables

UK Wide / Hybrid Environmental Consulting £ Competitive Permanent
Apply for this role

The Opportunity

A growing engineering consultancy is looking for a Principal Ecologist to provide senior technical leadership across a diverse portfolio of housing, infrastructure and renewables projects. This is a hands-on senior role with team management responsibility.

The Role

  • Provide senior technical leadership across ecology workstreams on housing, infrastructure and renewables projects
  • Lead EPS licence preparation and submission for complex schemes
  • Quality assure EIAs, BNG assessments and protected species reports
  • Manage a team of ecologists and support their professional development
  • Engage with clients, planning authorities and statutory consultees

About You

  • Degree in Ecology or a related environmental discipline
  • Two European Protected Species (EPS) survey licences — required
  • Full MCIEEM membership — required
  • Strong experience in BNG assessments using the Defra Metric
  • Full UK driving licence

What's on Offer

  • Competitive salary commensurate with experience
  • Flexible hybrid working
  • Genuine progression opportunities within a growing team
