Senior Ecologist — Bristol

Bristol / Hybrid Environmental Consulting £ Competitive Permanent
Ecology UKHab Bristol

A large, friendly and diverse ecology team within a leading global consultancy is seeking a Senior Ecologist in Bristol. You'll undertake UKHab and protected species surveys, contribute to PEAs, EcIAs, EPS licence applications, HRAs and EIA biodiversity chapters, and provide mentoring to junior colleagues.

The role involves client liaison, project management and input into fee quotations and tenders. Training and mentoring opportunities are available to support your career growth.

CIEEM membership required. Protected species licences or specialist skills highly desirable.
