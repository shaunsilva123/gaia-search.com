Why Gaia Search

Recruitment that actually feels different

Most recruitment agencies operate at volume, sending CVs, ticking boxes and moving on. Gaia Search was built to work differently. We are a specialist agency, not a generalist firm with an environmental division, and that distinction matters every time we pick up the phone.

Sector knowledge, not sector guesswork

Environmental and clean energy recruitment is all we do, across every discipline and level, from graduate to director. That focus means we understand your world, your career progression and what a genuinely good move looks like. You will not need to explain the basics.

A dedicated team, not a call centre

When you work with Gaia Search, you work with a close knit team that genuinely understands your brief, from the first conversation through to placement and beyond. No handovers between strangers and no chasing different people for updates. Just consistent, personal service built on listening properly to what you need.

No pressure, no scattergun

We will never send your CV anywhere without your permission, push you towards roles that are not the right fit or flood clients with unsuitable candidates simply to appear busy. Every introduction we make is carefully considered. That is how we maintain trust on both sides and why candidates return to us.

Built for the long term

The environmental and clean energy sector is close knit and well connected. Reputations, for both candidates and consultancies alike, travel quickly. We are invested in getting placements right, not simply getting them done. The people we place today are the hiring managers we work with tomorrow.
