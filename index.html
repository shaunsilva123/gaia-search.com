About Gaia Search

Built by specialists,
for the sector

Gaia Search was founded in 2023 by Shaun Silva — and Brendan — on a simple belief: that progress in environmental consulting and clean energy starts with the right people in the right roles.

Shaun spends his days speaking with clients and candidates across the UK, getting to know the people behind the CVs and the teams behind the projects. He cares deeply about the sector, and even more about making sure the right people end up in the right roles — not just the fastest fill.

Today, Gaia Search works with candidates and clients across ecology, EIA, flood risk, offshore wind, sustainability and beyond — placing people from graduate level through to director, at everything from independent boutiques to the UK's largest infrastructure consultancies.

Every great team has someone who keeps morale high. That's Brendan — self-appointed Chief Morale Officer, strict enforcer of walk schedules, and firm believer that no important decision should be made without a tennis ball in hand.
